Who We Are

Keeping up with the rising demand and maximizing our reach to customers.

MIDO international company, established in 1996 is a company that specialises in import / export business, mainly in the field of agriculture products and trade inside and outside Sudan. With experience in transport and trade within the country and, export services of products that include, but are not limited to; Peanuts, sesame, gum arabic and hibiscus the company has spacious storage facilities and reliable transportation throughout the country and outside to where customers find most comfortable. In addition, our company incorporated several branches to grapple the rising demand and furthermore established sister companies to ease the supply chain and logistics process:

Our China office, which was established in 1998
Our Turkey office, which was incorporated in 2014
Our sister company, Amirco International in Sudan, was established in 2020
Another sister company, Qimam Al Mustaqbal LLC in Dubai, found in 2021
