About Us

CONTINUOUSLY AIMING TO BECOME THE BEST AT WHAT WE DO

Who We Are

Keeping up with the rising demand and maximizing our reach to customers.

We are a group that specialises in the Agriculture processing stores, transportation solutions and import/export business established in 1996 based in Sudan with branches across the globe. Our company was established in 1996 as an extension of the family business at the time, we first began trading in commodities locally, offering a various array of products to the local markets and transporting across Sudan and abroad. Due to the emerging need of transportation solutions across the country, our company expanded into the logistics sector, working with the sudanese sugar company this gave our business an insight to into the commodities market and we began trading locally in oilseeds, mainly sesame and peanuts. After expanding into this market, we realised the potential and began investing in the market rapidly, and continued growing our experience and products range. We established our partner companies in China followed by Turkey, and then Dubai to keep up with the rising demand and to maximise our reach to customers. We work together with our partners through the whole supply chain process from farmers all the way to our customers, ensuring our customers get the best products and most importantly in time Our China office was established in 1998, our office in Turkey was incorporated in 2014, we established Amirco International company in sudan in 2020 and Qimam Al Mustaqbal LLC in Dubai in 2021 We offer a wide range of products including but not limited to; Peanuts ( Oil & Kernels ), Sesame seeds, Watermelon seeds, Gum arabic, Hibiscus, Pigeon peas and many more. We also continue to offer logistics solutions to our customers whether in Sudan or abroad. We value our reputation locally and globally, so the products and services we supply are always the top quality.
